Welcome to Our Club

The Big Data Artificial Intelligence Club at the University of Baghdad is a community of students passionate about exploring the future of data and AI. We organize workshops, projects, and events to help members learn, collaborate, and innovate together.

About Us

Founded in 2026, our club brings together students from diverse backgrounds who share a passion for technology, data science, and artificial intelligence. We believe in hands-on learning, teamwork, and building real-world solutions that can benefit our community.

Our Activities

  • Weekly study sessions on AI and Big Data topics
  • Hands-on coding workshops with Python and R
  • Guest lectures from industry experts
  • Collaborative hackathons and competitions
  • Research projects with faculty mentors

Why Join Us?

By joining our club, you’ll gain practical skills, meet like-minded peers, and have opportunities to showcase your work in conferences and competitions. Whether you’re a beginner or advanced, there’s a place for you here.
